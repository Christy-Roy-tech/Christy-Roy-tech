## 〔 01 〕 About

I design and build **intelligent robotic systems** — from low-level actuator control and sensor fusion to high-level task planning and perception pipelines. My work lives at the intersection of mechanical precision, real-time computation, and machine intelligence.

**Currently exploring:**
-  Whole-body loco-manipulation for dexterous mobile robots
-  Sim-to-real transfer using physics-informed learned policies
-  Fault-tolerant architectures for safety-critical deployments

> *"The robot is the most complex device humanity has ever attempted to build."* — Rodney Brooks
